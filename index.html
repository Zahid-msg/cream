Our Story

Nashra Flour Mill has been a trusted name in Uppala, Kerala for over 15 years. We take pride in our traditional milling techniques combined with modern technology to deliver the freshest and most aromatic flours and spices.

Our commitment to quality begins with carefully selecting the finest raw materials. We ensure that every product that leaves our mill meets the highest standards of purity and taste.

How we ensure the highest quality in every product
